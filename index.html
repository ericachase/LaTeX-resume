Impresum‌é

Impresum‌é is a resumé builder that takes all of the pain and hassle out of creating a beautiful and eye-catching resumé. Start by either taking a look at one of our prepared resume templates, or begin entering your own resumé infromation, such as contact information, education, work history, etc. Once you've entered your information, choose from one of our available templates. From there, the resume builder will insert your information into the template and show you a preview before you download or print your resumé.

How does it work? Impresumé begins with a library of resumé templates coded in LaTeX, a versatile and powerful document preparation system. The server reads your input into the LaTeX template, and compiles it with your information, taking all of the frustrating formatting work out of your hands.
